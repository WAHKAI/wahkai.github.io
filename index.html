About SANDA

Founded in 2019, SANDA is a professional bag and backpack manufacturer specializing in comprehensive OEM and ODM services. Operating from a 7,000-square-meter facility equipped with 135 advanced machines, our 7 dedicated production lines and 183 skilled workers ensure exceptional quality and stable output. Driven by a professional in-house team of 6 designers, we provide end-to-end manufacturing solutions. With a robust monthly capacity of over 50,000 pieces, SANDA has become a trusted supply chain partner for global brands and wholesalers. We proudly export to diverse markets worldwide.
